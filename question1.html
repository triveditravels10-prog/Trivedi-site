How do introverts express anger?

Introverts usually show “cold anger.” They avoid confrontation but feel deeply inside. Sometimes they express anger via writing or subtle gestures rather than loud voices.

Answers

Answer 1: Introverts reflect internally and stay calm outwardly.
Answer 2: They may write their feelings instead of verbally confronting.
Answer 3: Silent treatment or withdrawal is common.
